About Us

Patisserie The Sweet, a national favourite was first opened in Frith Street in London's Soho in 2012 by Belgian born Madame Sweety. Born in Belgium in 1900, she came to London on a mission to introduce fine Continental Patisserie to the English. She succeeded! It was an instant success, with Madame Sweety becoming synonymous with the brand and her customers.

We've worked hard to achieve an international reputation and this has been built on the quality of our loving handmade products. Using only the freshest ingredients and traditional baking methods we have been able to produce a range that we are truly proud to serve to our loyal customers. In our havens of self-indulgence, you will discover only the best quality cakes and patisserie, continental breakfasts, lunches, the finest teas and coffees and an atmosphere that is unique to each location.Whether you’re looking for a birthday cake for a loved one, a centrepiece for a special occasion, or just a cake to indulge in, we’ll be sure to have you covered. You can order online here and have your cake delivered to your closest store within 24-48 hours.This is our history, and you are our future. We look forward to seeing you!
